Who Are We?

Shenzhen Trading Co., Ltd. was established in 1995. We are located in Shenzhen Futian District. The fact that we are near Guangdong's largest international textile city. We have the luxury to obtain all varieties of fabrics. For the past 20 years, our team has been committed to the design, manufacture and export of clothing products. We have a professional team that will meet all your specified requirements and provide a high quality products at competitive prices.

WHY WORK WITH US?

Not only can we offer attractive pricing, and superb customer service. We will source high quality material, manufactured into your finished product by highly skilled workers. We look to turn your order around as quickly as possible, while keeping the standard of quality high. We also offer an all-inclusive price, where our total price includes production costs, shipping from the factory, sourcing the materials etc, with no hidden costs.

PAYMENT TERMS AND METHODS

If you are having a sample made, we will invoice you for the sample only. Once you have had the sample and wish to proceed with the bulk order, we require a 30% up-front payment, with the final 70% payable when we have completed the order and it is ready to be sent to you. We accept payment through bank transfer, and check. If you are paying by check, production will start once the payment has cleared in our account.
